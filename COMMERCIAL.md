## When you need approval

You must obtain **written approval** from Benjamin Arbibe before using this
software in any of the following:

- Proprietary or closed-source products
- Internal-only commercial systems that are not published under an OSI-approved license
- SaaS / hosted offerings where the product source is not open
- Redistribution as part of a paid binary or appliance without an OSI-approved license for that product

## How to request approval

Email **benjiar@gmail.com** with:

1. Your name / company
2. A short description of the product and how globe-chart will be used
3. Whether the consuming project is open source (and which license), or proprietary

Do not assume silence is approval. Use begins only after you receive written confirmation.
